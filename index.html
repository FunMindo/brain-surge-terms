Game Features & Content

Question Database. Brain Surge contains a built-in question database across categories including Animals, Science, Geography, Food & Cooking, Music, History, Sports, Technology, Literature, Maths & Logic, Movies, and more. Questions are provided for educational and entertainment purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, we make no guarantee that every answer or fact is free from error.

Categories & Unlocks. Two categories — Animals and Science — are unlocked by default. Additional categories are unlocked by earning XP through gameplay. No purchase is required to unlock any category. Special game modes (Sharp and Blitz) within each category may require spending in-game XP coins earned through normal play.

Daily Question of the Day. One bonus question is available per calendar day. Completion resets at midnight. This is an optional feature with no penalty for skipping.

Login Streaks. Daily login streaks are tracked locally on your device. A Streak Shield (watchable via rewarded ad) may protect one missed day. Streaks and all progress are stored on your device only and will be lost if you clear app data or uninstall the Game.

Lifelines. Each game session includes three lifelines (50/50, Skip, +10s). Each lifeline may be used once for free per session. After the free use, it becomes an ad-supported lifeline — watching a short rewarded video ad restores that lifeline for the current session.

XP & Ranks. XP is earned by answering questions correctly, maintaining streaks, and using the Double XP feature (via rewarded ad). Ranks are cosmetic only and confer no real-world value or monetary benefit.

Themes & Customisation. Decorative visual themes may be unlocked through XP or rewarded ads. Themes are cosmetic and do not affect gameplay.

Content Updates. We may add, modify, or remove questions, categories, features, or in-game content at any time without prior notice.
